# 🌊 Ocean Networks Canada (ONC)

### *Discover the ocean. Understand the planet.*

[Ocean Networks Canada](https://www.oceannetworks.ca), an initiative of the University of Victoria, operates world-leading ocean observatories that bring data to the surface from the Pacific, Atlantic, Arctic, and Southern Oceans. Our mission is to deliver next-generation digital infrastructure and ocean intelligence to address climate change, marine safety, and coastal resilience.

[**Explore Oceans 3.0**](https://data.oceannetworks.ca) | [**Documentation**](https://wiki.oceannetworks.ca/) | [**Our Mission**](https://www.oceannetworks.ca/about-onc/our-story/vision-mission-and-values/)


## 🚀 Featured Repositories

We maintain a variety of tools to help researchers, developers, and community scientists interact with our massive archive of real-time ocean data across multiple programming environments.

### 🐍 [api-python-client](https://github.com/OceanNetworksCanada/api-python-client)

**The primary Python gateway to ONC data.** This library wraps our Oceans 3.0 API, allowing you to discover locations, search for devices, and download over 120 different data products (including audio and video) with ease.
`Python` `Data Science` `API-Client`

### 📐 [api-matlab-client](https://github.com/OceanNetworksCanada/api-matlab-client)

**Native MATLAB integration.** Designed for researchers and engineers working in the MATLAB environment, this client provides direct access to the Oceans 3.0 API for seamless data ingestion and analysis.
`MATLAB` `Ocean Engineering` `Research`

### 🏗️ [Seismic-instrument-status](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://github.com/OceanNetworksCanada/Seismic-instrument-status)

**Monitoring Infrastructure Health.** A specialized utility for tracking the operational status of seismic instruments across our observatories. This ensures data integrity for critical monitoring of tectonic activity.
`Monitoring` `Seismology` `Infrastructure`

### 📓 [python-community-notebooks](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://github.com/OceanNetworksCanada/python-community-notebooks)

**Community-driven data science.** A collection of Jupyter Notebooks demonstrating real-world use cases, from plotting CTD data to analyzing hydrophone recordings.
`Jupyter Notebook` `Oceanography` `Open-Science`


## 🤝 How to Get Involved

We believe in **FAIR** (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable) data and open-source collaboration.

  * **Contribute:** We welcome pull requests for our client libraries and community notebooks\!
  * **Report Issues:** Found a bug? Open an issue in the respective repository or contact our [Service Desk](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://jira.oceannetworks.ca/servicedesk/customer/user/login).
  * **Indigenous Partnerships:** We are committed to [advancing Indigenous partnerships](https://www.oceannetworks.ca/science/community-based-monitoring/) in ocean science. Explore our community-based monitoring projects.
