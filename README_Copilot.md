# 🌊 Ocean Networks Canada  
**Real‑time ocean intelligence. Open data. Global impact.**

<p align="center">
  <img src="https://www.oceannetworks.ca/svg/logo.svg" width="180" alt="Ocean Networks Canada Logo">
</p>

We operate world‑leading ocean observatories that collect continuous, real‑time data from the Pacific, Atlantic, and Arctic coasts of Canada. Our mission is to **enable scientific discovery**, **support coastal communities**, **advance hazard monitoring**, and **empower global ocean stewardship** through open, accessible data.

## 🌐 What We Do

- 🌊 Operate cabled and community-based ocean observatories  
- 📡 Deliver real-time, high-quality ocean data through **Oceans 3.0**  
- 🧪 Support research in climate, ecosystems, geohazards, and ocean technology  
- 🤝 Collaborate with Indigenous communities, scientists, industry, and governments  
- 🌍 Contribute to global ocean observing and the UN Ocean Decade
